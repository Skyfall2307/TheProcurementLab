Welcome to ProcIQ

ProcIQ stands for Procurement Intelligence Quotient. It reflects a smarter, data-driven, and tech-powered approach to transforming procurement. This platform was born from over 15 years of experience in global procurement, digital transformation, and product management — helping organizations unlock strategic value through innovation.

ProcIQ brings together the worlds of sourcing, SaaS technology, GenAI, and supplier collaboration into a single destination. Here, you’ll find thought leadership, tools, and insights to help procurement professionals shift from transactional to transformational — and create measurable business impact.

About

I am a procurement and digital transformation leader with over 15 years of experience across SaaS platforms like Ariba, GEP, Ivalua, and Basware. I help organizations drive measurable value through Source-to-Pay (S2P) innovation and strategic technology adoption.

In my current role at OJC Consulting, I lead commercial strategy in the Nordics, managing presales and delivering procurement solutions that enhance operations and ROI. Previously, I directed global product strategy at Basware, mentoring a team of 12+ PMs and aligning our innovation roadmap with evolving client needs.

I bring hands-on expertise in deploying Ivalua, domain knowledge from GEP, and a passion for integrating GenAI and other emerging tech into procurement practices. My goal is to empower clients and teams to optimize performance and create sustainable value.
