Welcome to ProcIQ

ProcIQ stands for Procurement Intelligence Quotient. It reflects a smarter, data-driven, and tech-powered approach to transforming procurement. This platform was born from over 15 years of experience in global procurement, digital transformation, and product management — helping organizations unlock strategic value through innovation.

ProcIQ brings together the worlds of sourcing, SaaS technology, GenAI, and supplier collaboration into a single destination. Here, you’ll find thought leadership, tools, and insights to help procurement professionals shift from transactional to transformational — and create measurable business impact.

Women in Procurement Mentoring Program – Finland

This mentoring program is created for Women in Procurement in Finland to foster connection, growth, and community support. Whether you're early in your career or an experienced professional, this is an opportunity to learn, share, and elevate each other in a supportive network.

The program is free of charge and based entirely on willing participation. You can join as a mentor, a mentee, or as someone who’s open to either role.

We will kick off in August 2025 with a virtual introduction meeting to welcome everyone and set the tone for the experience. From there, mentor-mentee pairs will be encouraged to connect once per month for a dedicated 1:1 session.

In addition to the personal mentorship journey, we’ll also come together as a full group once per quarter in virtual team sessions, or in person to share insights, reflect, and build our network as a community.

Participation is entirely voluntary, with flexibility built in to accommodate each person's availability and goals. We're excited to welcome you!
