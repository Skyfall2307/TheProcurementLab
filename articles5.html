Where to Find Information for 2025 about S2P

April 27, 2025 • By Katarzyna Fonteyn

Staying on top of Source-to-Pay (#S2P) and Accounts Payable (#AP) technology trends is more important than ever.

If you're new to procurement or starting to explore the S2P or AP SaaS provider landscape, you might be wondering where to find reliable information. I’ve been there myself, and I know how overwhelming it can feel at first. That’s why I want to share a few trusted resources that can help you navigate the market more confidently and make better-informed decisions.

Here are some key resources:

1. Gartner Magic Quadrant for Source-to-Pay Suites (2025)

Gartner's 2025 Magic Quadrant evaluates S2P vendors based on their ability to execute and completeness of vision.

2. Gartner Magic Quadrant for Accounts Payable Invoice Automation (2025)

In 2025, Gartner introduced a dedicated Magic Quadrant for Accounts Payable Invoice Automation (APIA) solutions, reflecting the growing importance of AP-specific tools.

3. Gartner Peer Insights: Source-to-Pay Suites

Gartner Peer Insights offers user reviews and ratings for various S2P solutions, providing practical feedback on implementation experiences and vendor performance.

4. Ardent Partners Research: The State of Source-to-Pay Digitization 2024

This report presents insights from 382 procurement executives worldwide, examining the current state of S2P digitization and the role of procurement leaders in driving business resilience.

5. 2025 Supplier Management Technology Advisor

Ardent Partners' 2025 report evaluates the top supplier management solution providers, assisting procurement leaders in selecting the best-fit solutions based on their unique requirements and budgets.

6. The Hackett Group Inc. Insights: 2025 CPO Agenda: Gen AI Takes Center Stage

The Hackett Group's 2025 agenda explores how Chief Procurement Officers are leveraging generative AI to address increasing workloads and expectations, emphasizing the transformative impact of AI on procurement functions.

7. Spend Matters SolutionMap: Spring 2025 Rankings

Spend Matters' Spring 2025 SolutionMap provides a comprehensive ranking of over 100 procurement technology providers across 16 categories, including S2P, P2P, and supplier management.
