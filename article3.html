What is Process Mining in S2P?

31st of March 2025 • By Katarzyna Fonteyn

I was familiar with the benefits of process mining across different business processes, but it wasn’t until about a year ago that I truly deep-dived into its value for Source-to-Pay (S2P).

Process mining uses real event logs from S2P systems to reconstruct and visualize how processes actually run—not just how they’re documented. This creates true end-to-end transparency across the entire S2P cycle.

Where It Helps in the S2P Lifecycle

Process mining doesn’t just give high-level insights — it dives deep into every step of the S2P process, revealing where bottlenecks, non-compliance, and value leakage occur.

1. Sourcing

Common challenges: Lack of visibility into cycle times, RFx management, or delays in contract creation.

How process mining helps:

  • Analyzes duration of sourcing events (RFx to contract)
  • Highlights delays and variations by category, buyer, or region
  • Detects skipped or out-of-sequence steps

2. Procure-to-Order

Common challenges: Maverick buying, slow approvals, and order errors.

How process mining helps:

  • Identifies POs created after invoices or not linked to contracts
  • Detects long cycle times in PO creation and approval flows
  • Uncovers repetitive manual tasks and unnecessary rework

3. Invoice-to-Pay

Common challenges: Late payments, duplicate invoices, missed early payment discounts.

How process mining helps:

  • Tracks full invoice lifecycle (receipt to payment)
  • Flags blocked invoices and manual intervention causes
  • Analyzes payment behavior and its financial impact

Key Business Benefits

  • Visibility: Reveals true process flows across systems, regions, and teams
  • Compliance: Identifies policy violations like PO splitting or off-contract spend
  • Efficiency: Reduces sourcing and payment cycles, increases automation
  • Cost savings: Avoids late fees, captures early discounts, reduces manual effort
  • Performance improvement: Benchmarks KPIs like touchless invoice rate and PO compliance
  • Root cause analysis: Uses AI to understand not just what happened, but why—and how to fix it

Process mining transforms S2P into a living, breathing system you can monitor, diagnose, and continuously improve.

It empowers Procurement, Finance, and Shared Services teams to work smarter—with data, not guesswork.
